Pasarakat

PASARAKAT (Pasar Rakyat Timur) is a service company in eastern Indonesia focus in trade and retail industry. Pasarakat offers the high quality products of Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) that have received recognition from the local and central government. The products that we offered are food and beverages, crafts, traditional motif fabrics, and those originally handmade of local entrepreneurs. Pasarakat was planned to reach all categories of Indonesia people.
